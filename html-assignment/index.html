About Me

Hello!

My name is Brooklyn Smith! I was born on July 1st, the same day my home nation of Canada gained its independence. I enjoy playing video games such as Chrono Trigger, Xenoblade Chronicles, and the Cat in the Hat for the GameBoy Advance (I'm in second place on the speedrun.com leaderboards for it!), coding, and practicing my Lightsaber skills with my best friend, Riley. I am hoping that IMD is the right fit for me and look forward to seeing what it has to offer!
